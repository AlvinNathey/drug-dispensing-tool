About us

MyDispenser unit is an integral part of the healthcare system, playing a crucial role in ensuring access to medications for individuals in need.

Beyond its primary function of providing pharmaceutical drugs, MyDispenser unit goes beyond traditional boundaries by forging numerous partnerships and collaborations with various stakeholders to make the world a better place.


By establishing strong alliances,MyDispenser unit gains access to a diverse array of resources, expertise, and knowledge that ultimately benefits the patients and the broader community.

The unit's aim to make the world a better place is multi-faceted. Firstly, it strives to enhance patient outcomes by ensuring the availability of safe, effective, and affordable medications.

Through partnerships with pharmaceutical companies, it can negotiate favorable pricing and expand its medication inventory, catering to a wider range of patient needs.

Secondly, the drug dispensing unit actively engages in research collaborations with academic institutions and research organizations.

By participating in clinical trials and research studies, it contributes to the advancement of medical knowledge, leading to improved treatment options and innovative therapies
